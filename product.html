Our Spack device is equipped with 2 sensors: an accelerometer and a gyroscopic. Indeed, thanks to the linear acceleration and angular velocity sent by our sensors, this data will be pre-processed by a noise filter. The filtered frequencies will be sent to our model of neural networks which will do in-depth analysis and make relevant predictions. It will show the short-term and long-term consequences of sustaining a bad posture. Personalized reccomendations will be avaible to the user, and all of this will allow you to see real progress overtime!

With its 72 hours battery life, you don't need to worry about missing a using it for a few days straight. You can go and work from anywhere for the weekend while having the confidence that your posture will not be affected. It has a USB-C port and also supports wireless charging. Thanks to the fast-charge feature, with 10 minutes of charging you can have 4 hours of usage. You will never have to think about Spack running out of power!

Spack combined with the app will give access to powerful data analysis tools. You will have real-time sensory feedback, predictions for your back's health based on your daily habits, all time posture graphs and many more! You will also have access to improvment graphs. They will show you how much you have enhanced your posture over time and what would have happened if no actions were taken: back pain, bad curvature of the spine and long-term consequences such as scoliosis or lordosis. Good news is you can avoid all these things to happen!

The chip is made out of the latest technology in composite materials. It is as solid as steel, but light like a feather!
